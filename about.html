About

Bhaskar Ahuja

PhD researcher in Space Domain Awareness specializing in passive radar, bistatic tracking, orbit determination, and radio telescope based radar systems.

Bhaskar works on space object tracking with radio telescope receivers, multi-sensor data fusion, advanced filtering methods including EKF and UKF, and signal processing for space surveillance.
