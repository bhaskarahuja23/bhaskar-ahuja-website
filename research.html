Research

Space sensing with radar systems

The site is structured so you can expand each research theme with project summaries, collaborators, datasets, and publication links.

Area 01

Space Situational Awareness

Radar-based tracking and characterization of resident space objects, debris, and satellites under challenging observation geometries.

Area 02

Multistatic Radar Architectures

Distributed sensing and bistatic receiver concepts, including radio telescopes as high-sensitivity receiving assets for GEO tracking.

Area 03

State Estimation

Position and velocity estimation during low-thrust maneuvers using radar data, filtering methods, and sensor network geometry.

Area 04

Machine Learning for Tracking

Physics-aware neural networks and resilient classification methods for radar, SAR, and satellite tracking workflows.
