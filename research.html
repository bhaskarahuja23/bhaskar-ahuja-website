Research and publications

Space Domain Awareness with radar systems

Research across passive radar, bistatic tracking, radio telescope receivers, orbit determination, multi-sensor fusion, and machine learning for space surveillance.

Area 01

Space Domain Awareness

Tracking and monitoring resident space objects using radar measurements, orbit determination, and trajectory prediction workflows.

Area 02

Passive and bistatic radar

Radio telescope based receiver concepts, passive radar architectures, and bistatic sensing for space debris monitoring.

Area 03

State estimation and fusion

Multi-sensor data fusion and advanced filtering techniques including EKF and UKF for satellite tracking during maneuvers.

Area 04

Beamforming and signal processing

Antenna arrays, digital beamforming, and signal processing methods for array-based sensing and surveillance applications.

2025

URSI Asia-Pacific Radio Science Meeting (AP-RASC)

Conference publication on entropy-based Bayesian model averaging for classification resilience against adversarial perturbations using SAR images.

2024

4th IAA International Conference on Space Situational Awareness

Presented work on improving satellite position and velocity estimation during low-thrust maneuvers using multi-bistatic radar and the Unscented Kalman Filter; received 3rd Best Student Paper Award.

2024

International Radar Conference

Presented research on space object state estimation during constant thrust maneuver using distributed sensor networks.

2024

Certificate of participation at the Mathematics Department of the University of Pisa.

2023

International Summer Schools
